PICTURE INFORMATION
Doc Hudson (also known as The Fabulous Hudson Hornet, Hud, or simply Doc) is an anthropomorphic 1951 Hudson Hornet who appears in the 2006 Pixar film Cars. In the film, Doc is the medical doctor and local judge for Radiator Springs
Lightning McQueen is a legendary Piston Cup racer, who has won 7 Piston Cups since his debut in 2005. He is the main protagonist of Disney/Pixar's Cars franchise, and appears as Mater's sidekick in Mater's Tall Tales
Sir Tow Mater, better known as Mater, is the deuteragonist of the Cars franchise. In the films, he is a major character in Cars, the main protagonist of Cars 2 and a supporting character in Cars 3. In the TV shows, he is the titular protagonist of his own Tall Tales series, one of the main characters of Tales From Radiator Springs and one of the two main protagonists of Cars on the Road.
